Find roadside stalls and farm shops
across the country with GoGetGreen's free app!

GoGetGreen is a free app that gives you an overview of roadside stalls and farm shops across the country. We show the way to local products and raw materials in season. When you download the app, you become part of our community of over 15,500 active users who support locally and live more sustainably. 650 have already created their own market on the app and you can also become a micro farmer for your neighborhood by selling your profits from the garden!

With us, there is room for new innovative production methods that do not yet have a state-controlled labeling, but still have a sustainable perspective and we want even more enthusiasts to have the opportunity to start their own business, using our platform and community.

You do not need to be the owner of a garden or a physical roadside stall to sell on the app. For example, you are good at growing herbs on the windowsill, so you can also create your own digital market on the app and sell them.

Sell your produce

Be it vegetables and fruits, or even homemade sweet jams and ciders, you can sell any surplus produce on our app.

Buy in season locally

Become more sustainable and zero-waste, and even help the climate by shopping in season and locally.

Teach and learn new skills

Launching soon - workshops and lessons about a more sufficent and zero-waste lifestyle for the community by the community. Join classes held by our users willing to share their knowledge.
